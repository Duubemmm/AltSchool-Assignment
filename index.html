About Me

Reasons for joining AltSchool Africa

  • I joined Altschool Africa to gain valuable industry experience through hands-on learning and building real-world projects.
  • To have the relevant updated skills, tools and technologies for Frontend engineering.
  • To collaborate better as a team player and be part of a tech community.
  • AltSchool Africa prepares students for the job market by including projects, assignments and assessments that mimic real-world challenges. This practical exposure is significant to my learning journey as it bridges the gap between education and employment.
  • Learning from experienced tutors and industry professionals with a structured curriculum is a major boost and a significant advantage which is crucial for my professional development.

My Goals for AltSchool Africa

  • To be a well grounded Frontend Engineer in the basics, concepts and implementation of programming in Software Engineering.
  • To be able to design, build, maintain, develop and test softwares with globally standardised procedures and principles.
  • By the end of the programme, I want to have a robust portfolio of projects that demonstrates my ability to tackle real-world challenges and develop functional, aesthetically pleasing websites and applications.
  • Earn a prestigious diploma certificate from AltSchool Africa School of Engineering
  • I intend to leverage the duration of this programme to connect with fellow students, instructors and industry professional so as to build a strong professional network which is crucial for my career development as it opens doors to mentorship, collaboration and job opportunities.
